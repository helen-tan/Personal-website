Jul 2019 - Nov 2019

During our internship in Shenzhen, a group of friends and I came up with an idea for a new type of computer peripheral - a hybrid between a SSD enclosure and a USB-C hub. In partnership with Flujo Technology, we crowdfunded the idea on Kickstarter, within 24 hours and with the support of 249 backers.

Seeed Studio (NUS Overseas Colleges - Shenzhen)
Jan 2019 - Jul 2019

In my 3rd year at NUS, I had the opportunity to take part in an overseas internship program called the NUS Overseas Colleges. I went to Shenzhen, China, where I interned at chinese IoT start-up called Seed Studio as a content marketer. Focusing mainly on technical writing for the company's blog, I wrote quick-start guides on setting up simple projects with Arduino, user stories, and articles on printed-circuit board theory.
On weekends, I enjoyed exploring Shenzhen and immersed myself in its tech culture. On public holidays, I seized the chance to tour neighbouring cities of Hong Kong, Fuzhou, and Zhangzhou.
